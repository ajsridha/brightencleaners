Meet your new dry cleaner.

We apply loving care to your clothes, our staff, and our customers. Best of all, it doesn't cost the earth.

Quality & Reliable Cleaning

Every visit to Brighten Cleaners feels as if you’ve repurchased your clothing. Your clothing will come back feeling brand new! Our high-quality dry cleaning removes the toughest of stains, allowing you to enjoy your fresh garments..

Eco-friendly

We use Eco-friendly dry cleaning technology, hydrocarbon cleaning method, and have moved away from using the cleaning solvent perchloroethylene (perc). What better way to contribute to your environment by reducing your own carbon footprints!

Customer Oriented

We treat your clothes with the care they deserve. We value our customers highly and customer satisfaction is always our number one priority.
